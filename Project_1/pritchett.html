Pritchett family

Older dad Jay deals with a few embaressing moments as strangers mistake his second wife Gloria for his daughter. Manny, Gloria's son with her first husband, wasn't always a fan of Jay. So much so that he chose the first song for their wedding, Beauty and the Beast! And although he loves, loud and loving mum, Gloria, he's not a big fan of the cute names she calls him in public! Jay and Gloria go on to have son, Joe, together. Joe adds another layer of hilarity to the Prothett family and brings a little bit of shame when he steals the priest's wallet. And then there's Stella, a destructive French Bulldog, who Gloria thinks Jay shows more love to than her.
