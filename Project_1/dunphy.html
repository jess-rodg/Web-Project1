Dunphy family

Stay-at-home-mum turned CEO Claire, and self-proclaimed cool dad Phil, balance family life with three very different kids. Intelligent Alex has her sights set on a scientific breakthrough, Hailey can't really decide what to do with her life and after dropping out of college finds herself working for a fashion company and starting to get recognition online. When we discover that Alex has a sizable fan base, just like Hailey, Alex tells Hailey that someday, your fans are going to work for my fans. Finally, we get to the youngest Dunphy family member. Luke, easily distracted and mischievous, seems to forever undertake questionable actions like jumping on a trampoline wearing only his underwear with a box on his head and getting his head stuck in the bannister!
