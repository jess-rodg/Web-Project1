Pritchett-Tucker family

Rule follower, Lawyer Mitchell and his excentric, farm raised husband Cam are raising daughter Lily through her teen years, trying to navigate all the experiences that come with being a teenage girl, ones often they can't relate to. Lily loves her dads, but hates when she has to judge their who wears it best deabates when they put on the same outfit before going out. Renting out the top floor of their house leads to a whole host of interesting people coming into their lives, from a christian rock band to Cam's newly released from prison sister. The pritchett-Tucker household is very loud but never short on love!
