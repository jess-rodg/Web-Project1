Showing the realities of family life, Modern Family follows three very different families from the perspective of an unseen documentry film-maker. Jay and his younger wife gloria tackle bringing up two boys at very different stages of life. Jays daughter Claire and exentric husband Phil battle teenage years of their three very individual kids. Jays son Mitchel and husband Cam navigate bringing up their adopted daughter Lily and the possibility of more kids. The three families come together to light up your TV screens and leave you wanting more.

Show success

Modern Family quickly became a household favourite when released in 2019, with the first season recieving a 100% rating from critics on rottern tomatoes. The show has since recieved 82 emmy nominations, winning 22 of the awards in a wide array of catagories, showing the strength of the show from production through to cast. With 14.5 million watchers tuning into the first episode of season 3, the shows success was clear from the very beggining.
